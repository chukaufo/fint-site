Our Mission

Privacy isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Fint was built on a single principle: your financial data belongs to you. Not to advertisers. Not to analytics platforms. Not even to us. The developer of Fint cannot see your spending, because there is no server to send it to.

Everything lives on your device. If you choose to sync across your Apple devices, it travels encrypted through your personal iCloud, a private tunnel only you can open.

But Fint is more than privacy. It's your private ledger: a commitment to understand your habits, honour your budgets, and build a healthier relationship with money. Every receipt you scan and every budget you set is a small act of financial self-awareness.

What's inside

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

01

Receipt scanning

Point your camera at any receipt. Fint extracts the store name, total, and date instantly using on-device OCR with no upload required.

02

Smart budget tracking

Set daily, weekly, and monthly budgets per category. Get proactive alerts at 50%, 75%, 90%, and 100% so you're never caught off guard.

03

Recurring bills

Log your subscriptions and bills once. Fint auto-records them on their due date and reminds you two days before.

04

Spending insights

Visual charts across daily, weekly, and yearly views reveal your real spending patterns, not averages but your actual data.

05

Fint Wrapped

Every December 31st, get a full year in review: top categories, biggest purchases, best streaks, and your quietest month.

06

Multi-currency support

Travel or live across regions. Each receipt and budget snapshots its currency at save time, keeping historical data accurate forever.

Open source by default

Fint's source code is publicly available. Read it, audit it, and verify our privacy claims yourself. Trust shouldn't require faith. It should be earned through transparency.
